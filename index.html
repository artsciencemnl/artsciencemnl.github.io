Robotics & Creative Science for Kids in Manila

At Art & Science MNL, we introduce children ages 5–12 to robotics, coding, and creative science. Through fun, project-based lessons, kids learn teamwork, problem-solving, and confidence while building their own robots.

Programs

Ages 5–12 · 10 sessions per course · 50 minutes per session · Max 8 learners

Level 1 — Explorers (5–7)

  • Outcomes: sequencing, basic sensors, teamwork, safe tool use
  • Builds: line-following bug, light-reactive nightlight
  • Prerequisites: none

Level 2 — Builders (8–10)

  • Outcomes: loops & logic, gearing, prototyping, troubleshooting
  • Builds: obstacle-avoiding rover, mini-drawbot
  • Prerequisites: Level 1 or similar experience

Level 3 — Inventors (11–12)

  • Outcomes: planning & testing, sensors integration, simple autonomy
  • Builds: sumo bot, delivery bot challenge
  • Prerequisites: Level 2 or similar experience

Borrow-not-buy: we lend all robots/kits during class—no purchase required.
