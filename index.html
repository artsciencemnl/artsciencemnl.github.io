Robotics & Creative Science for Kids in Makati

At Art & Science MNL, we introduce children ages 5 to 12 to robotics, coding, and creative science. We use the friendly micro:bit and the DeskPi MicroCar to teach real problem solving through fun builds and challenges.

Micro:bit + MicroCar Coding Lab

Ages 5 to 12 · 10 sessions · 1 hour each · Max 8 learners

What kids learn

  • Block coding with MakeCode: logic, loops, variables, events
  • Motor control, gearing basics, and real world sensors
  • Debugging, teamwork, and planning a simple robot strategy

Projects you will build

  • Safety light and horn (inputs and outputs)
  • Follow the Line MicroCar (calibration and testing)
  • Obstacle Avoidance Bot (distance sensing)
  • Night Drive challenge (light sensor)
  • Delivery race (waypoints and timing)

Weekly arc

  • Weeks 1 to 2: Micro:bit basics, inputs and outputs
  • Weeks 3 to 4: Motors, motion, gearing
  • Weeks 5 to 6: Sensing and reactions
  • Weeks 7 to 8: Simple autonomy and strategy
  • Week 9: Debug and refine
  • Week 10: Showcase Day

All materials and robots are provided for in-class use. No purchase required.
